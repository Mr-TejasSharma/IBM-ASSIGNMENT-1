International Business Machines Corporation (IBM)

IBM is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Armonk, New York. It is one of the world's largest employers and a major research organization in the field of computing.

History

Founded in 1911 as the Computing-Tabulating-Recording Company, it was renamed IBM in 1924. It pioneered the development of mainframe computers and continues to innovate in AI and quantum computing.

Products

  • IBM Watson (AI)
  • IBM Cloud
  • IBM Z Mainframes
  • Quantum Computing

Research and Development

IBM Research has made significant contributions in AI, quantum computing, cybersecurity, and nanotechnology. It holds the record for most U.S. patents generated by a business for decades.
